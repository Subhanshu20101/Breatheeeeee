Probable Causes: - URTIs are usually caused by viruses, such as rhinovirus, adenovirus, and coronavirus. - In some cases, bacteria, such as Streptococcus pyogenes (group A streptococcus), can also cause URTIs.

Prevention: - Frequent hand washing and avoiding close contact with people who have respiratory infections can help prevent the spread of the viruses and bacteria that cause URTIs. - Avoiding exposure to secondhand smoke and other irritants can also help reduce the risk of developing URTIs.
