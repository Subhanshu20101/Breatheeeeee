Probable Causes: - Pneumonia can be caused by a variety of bacteria, viruses, and other microorganisms. - Streptococcus pneumoniae is a common cause of bacterial pneumonia. - Influenza viruses, respiratory syncytial virus (RSV), and adenoviruses are common causes of viral pneumonia.

Prevention: - Vaccines are available to prevent some of the most common causes of bacterial pneumonia, including Streptococcus pneumoniae and Haemophilus influenzae type B (Hib). - Good hygiene, such as washing hands frequently and covering the mouth and nose when coughing or sneezing, can help prevent the spread of the viruses and bacteria that cause pneumonia.
